ABOUT ME

Renee Penland is an emerging Web Designer. She recently graduated from Brightpoint Community College with an AAS in Web Design. She is a member of the Phi Theta Kappa, the Honorary Society for Community College where she also serves as Secretary for the group. Renee is focused on empowering people thru the dissemination of information. Helping people is the motivation behind her 14 years in customer service in the trucking industry, five years of teamwork in the Amazon e-commerce warehouse setting, and her educational background in Library Science.

Renee has a Bachelor of Arts degree from Randolph Macon College in Ashland, VA where she completed a Major in History, a minor in Classics, and a Women's Studies focus. Renee was a member of Phi Beta Kappa, the Honorary Society in college. She spent two years working towards a master's degree in library and information science at the School of Information and Library Science at the University of North Carolina in Chapel Hill.

Now, Renee wants to expand her ability to help people by designing dynamic webpages for government, non-profits or businesses. Renee is looking for an entry-level job where she can practice the skills she has learned. She would like to work as part of a team that gives her an opportunity to grow.

Renee has passed the CIW User Interface Certification and plans to pass the CIW Advanced HTML and CSS3 Certification. Renee enjoys coding in HTML and CSS3. She also has limited knowledge of JavaScript. Renee has completed all 7 of the Google Courses that comprise the Google UX Design Certificate, giving her insight to the UX Design process.

Renee's greatest strengths are listening, empathizing, and writing. She is passionate about accomplishing goals and meeting deadlines.
